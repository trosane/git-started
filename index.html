Tina Belcher

Tina is a character from the FX animated series Bob's Burgers. She is the oldest of her siblings, Gene and Louise. Tina is an awkward teenage girl that likes horses and writing "erotic friend fiction". She groans a lot when under pressure, and speaks in a very monotone voice. She is voiced by Dan Mintz.
